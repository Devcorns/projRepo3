Conservation of Plants & Animals
What are Flora and Fauna?

Some animals and plants typically belong to a particular area.

Plants and animals of a particular area are known as the flora and fauna of that area.

Endemic species

A Species is a group of populations which are capable of interbreeding.

Members of a species have common characteristics.

Species of plants and animals which are found exclusively in a particular area are called endemic species.

They are not found anywhere else.

A particular type of animal or plant may be endemic to a zone, a state or a country.

Example of Endemic species

What factors lead to make a species endemic?

Well, humans are the main reason.

The destruction of their habitat, increasing human population, and the introduction of a new species may affect the natural habitat of endemic species and endanger their existence.

Which Species are called Endangered?

Species whose numbers are diminishing to a level that they might face extinction are known as endangered species.

Survival of some animals has become difficult because of disturbances in their natural habitat.

You may be wondering if there is any record of endangered species.

Well, the Red Data Book is a document which keeps a record of all endangered animals and plants.

What is Migration?

What does the term migration mean?

Migration is the phenomenon of the movement of a species from its own habitat to some other habitat, for a particular time period, every year. This is for a specific purpose like breeding or avoiding climatic conditions.

Solutions to the problem of deforestation

We get many things from forests.

Gum, resin, jute and paper are important products.

Can you think of any alternatives available for paper?

What would happen if we had no wood?

In response to this, the recycling of paper is the simplest answer.

Paper can be recycled five to seven times.

By recycling paper, not only trees, but also energy and water may be saved.

Thus, we should reduce, reuse, and recycle paper.

But recycling paper is a measure just for controlling deforestation temporarily.

Can you suggest any permanent solution to the problem of deforestation?

Yes, it is reforestation.

Reforestation is the restocking of destroyed forests by planting new trees.
