Do you take public transport? You’ll get to Wroclavia via 28 different public transport routes!

Wroclavia is directly accessible from over seven city districts. You’ll get here by 13 different trams or 15 buses.

Public transport not enough? Wroclavia is located 2 minutes by foot from the Main Railway Station and the Wrocław Coach Terminal is actually inside the building, below ground. You don’t even have to live in Wrocław to get to us quickly and easily.

You love to cycle? Wroclavia’s just the place for you!

Cycling enthusiasts will be thrilled to know that there are 1,300 m of new bike lanes, 400 bicycle parking spots, and at least 3 rent-a-bike stations in close vicinity.

And that’s it folks! PGS Software encourages you to go green and hop on a bike. We even organise company bicycle trips and take part in cycling competitions.

You can even walk here!

The HQ’s city centre location means that you’re never more than a few steps away from Wrocław’s most attractive destinations.

  • The Main Railway Station? A mere 2 min. walk away!
  • Downtown? 15 min. by foot.
  • Musical Theatre Capitol district? Less than 10 min. walking distance.
